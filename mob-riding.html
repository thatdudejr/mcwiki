    • Riding Mobs

    In Minecraft, some mobs can be ridden by players, offering a unique and often speedy mode of transportation. Riding mobs can be both fun and practical.

    Mobs You Can Ride

    Some mobs that you can ride in Minecraft include:

    • Horses
    • Pigs (with a carrot on a stick)
    • Striders (in the Nether)
    • Boats (on water)
