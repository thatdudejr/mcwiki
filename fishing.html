    • Fishing

    Fishing is a popular activity in Minecraft that allows players to catch various fish and items from bodies of water. It's a relaxing and rewarding pastime in the game.

    Fishing Rewards

    When you go fishing in Minecraft, you can obtain the following items:

    • Fish (Salmon, Cod, Pufferfish)
    • Treasure (Enchanted Books, Name Tags)
    • Junk (Seaweed, Leather Boots)
