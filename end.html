The End in Minecraft

The End is a mysterious and challenging dimension in Minecraft, home to the Ender Dragon and Endermen. It's the final destination for many players in the game.

Key Features

Some key features of The End include:

  • The Ender Dragon
  • End Gateways
  • End Cities
  • Chorus Plants
  • Shulkers
